Teriyaki Pork Belly

Indulge in the rich and savory flavors of teriyaki pork belly. This dish combines with the umami sweetness of hoisin sauce with the rich crispy texture of pork belly, creating a delightful culinary experience. Perfect for a weekend gathering or a special family dinner, this recipe delivers an irresistible taste that will leave your guests wanting more!.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork belly, skin scored
  • 1/4 cup of hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic

Instructions

  1. Marinate the pork: In a bowl, mix the hoisin sauce, soy sauce and minced garlic. Coat the pork belly in the marinated cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight for the best flavor.
  2. Grill the pork: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Place the marinated pork belly on the grill cooking for about 6-8 minutes per side or until the pork is crispy and cooked through. Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  3. Serve: Fluff rice for an example, with a fork and divide it among plates. Top with slices of grilled teriyaki pork belly and garnish with fresh cilantro if desired. Enjoy your delicious meal!
