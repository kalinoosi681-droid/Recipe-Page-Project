Best Taco Casserole Recipe

This hearty taco casserole brings all the bold flavors of tacos into one comforting dish, with layers of seasoned ground beef, crunchy tortilla chips, gooey cheese and zesty toppings. Its a crowd pleasing recipe that is simple to prepare. You can even substitute with ground turkey or low-fat diary for a lighter version without losing any of the deliciousness!. Perfect for family dinners, potlucks, or game night, this casserole is best served with salsa, fresh chips and a crisp green salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 small flour tortillas, cut into strips
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the taco seasoning to the beef along with a little water (as per the seasoning packet instructions). Stir well and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the cooked beef, black beans, corn, and salsa. Mix until everything is well incorporated.
  5. In a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, layer half of the tortilla strips on the bottom.
  6. Spread half of the beef mixture over the tortilla layer.
  7. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese over the beef mixture.
  8. Repeat the layers with the remaining tortilla strips, beef mixture, and cheese.
  9. Bake uncovered for 20-50 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from oven, sprinkle with green onions, jalapenos and cilantro.
  11. Serve warm with salsa, extra chips and a green salad.
